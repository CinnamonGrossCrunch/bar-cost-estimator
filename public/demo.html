Service Cost Estimate Widget - Embed Demo

Method 1: Script Tag (Shadow DOM)

Add this single line to your HTML:

<script src="https://your-vercel-domain.vercel.app/embed.js"></script>

Method 2: iFrame

Add this iframe to your HTML:

<iframe 
  src="https://your-vercel-domain.vercel.app/embed" 
  width="100%" 
  height="700" 
  frameborder="0"
  title="Service Cost Estimate">
</iframe>
